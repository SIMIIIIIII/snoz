GAME MECHANICS:
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Spawning:
---------
• Tracks regular fruits eaten via 'fruitsEaten' counter
• Every 8 regular fruits eaten → spawn 1 bonus fruit
• Spawns at random location in playable area (avoids walls)
• Broadcasts bonusFruitSpawned(X Y) to all bots

Consumption:
------------
• Snake moves to bonus fruit cell
• Awards +5 points (global score)
• Awards +5 points (individual snake score)
• Displays: "[Color] ate a BONUS fruit (+5 pts)!"
• Snake grows by 5 segments
• Bonus fruit removed from grid
• Rankings updated

State Tracking:
---------------
• Label: bonusfruit
• Status: alive (true/false)
• Counter: nBfruits (tracks active bonus fruits)
• Stored in State.items record
